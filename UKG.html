UKG

Age Group: 4.6 to 5.6 Years

UKG

Our Montessori III (UKG) curriculum meets the requirements of CBSE/ICSE/NEP 2020. On language and literature front our program aims at developing the competence in talking, listening and becoming readers and writers with growing vocabulary and increased fluency to express their thoughts and to convey the meaning to the listeners. Using phonics, understanding the formation of words and thus sentences for the development of good writing skills is an integral part of language development. This program covers development of pre-mathematical skills related to comparison, sorting, matching, ordering, sequencing and counting. Four basic mathematical operations are also introduced at the UKG level. Interactive digital smart class activities make learning extremely enjoyable for the children.

Key areas of development are:

  • Fluency in spoken language
  • Concept Application skills
  • Reading & Writing
  • Maths Concepts and applications
  • Refinement of fine and gross motor skills
  • Creative Expressions
  • Rhythm and Movement
  • General Knowledge
  • Logical thinking
