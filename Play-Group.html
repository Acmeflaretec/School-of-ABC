Play Group

Age Group: 2.6 to 3.6 Years

Play Group

Play Group children enjoy the freedom of choice of abundance of activity materials under the guidance of well trained and caring teachers. Indoor and Outdoor play equipment help them to develop physical coordination and gross motor skills. Lot of emphasis is given in developing the communication skills through the participation of various thematic activities all-round the year. Children are also engaged in learning phonics, reading and writing skills.

Key areas of development are:

  • Communications Skills
  • Recognition Skills
  • Pattern Tracing skills
  • Self Help skills
  • Good Habit
  • Cognitive skills
  • Application skills
  • Numeracy & Alphabet
  • Creativity skills
