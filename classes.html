School Classes

Engaging classes for preschool, LKG, and UKG students with hands-on learning, thematic activities, and interactive smart lessons

Preschool

Our preschool program at School of ABC is designed to provide young learners with a nurturing, stimulating, and enriching environment that fosters holistic development. We follow a hands-on, minds-on learning approach inspired by Montessori principles and aligned with the Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) framework of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020.

LKG (Lower Kindergarten)

In our LKG (Lower Kindergarten) class, we build upon the foundation laid in preschool. Children are introduced to structured learning experiences that enhance their cognitive abilities, language skills, and social development. Our curriculum integrates thematic activities and interactive smart class sessions to prepare children for a smooth transition to formal schooling.

UKG (Upper Kindergarten)

The UKG (Upper Kindergarten) program at School of ABC is designed to prepare children for primary education. Emphasis is placed on refining literacy and numeracy skills, fostering independent learning, and promoting critical thinking. Through a blend of classroom activities, life skills education, and thematic learning, children in UKG develop confidence, creativity, and readiness for future academic challenges.
