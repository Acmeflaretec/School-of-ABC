About us

The School Of ABC is a new generation premium child care and preschool chain. It is established with the aim of providing a holistic approach to child development were rooted in the Montessori way of Early Childhood Learning and it strives to be the preferred parenting partner

The school of ABC is a special place where students, teachers and non teaching staff work together with a common mission – ‘to prepare our children to be productive citizens’ We specialize in early childhood education through the Hands on Minds on Learning approach. We are moving successfully by spreading smiles and creating sweet memories in the minds of our dear students and their parents

What we Offer

Our teaching approach is unique and activity based on a blended program consisting of Montessori activities, thematic activities, classroom activities, smart class activities and life skills education along with plenty of video lessons with Progress Tracker covering the curriculum.. It is aligned with NEP2020. This unique method is activity based, fun filled and totally stress free for your child.

Curriculum

Application-Oriented Learning

Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to encompass a blend of Montessori, thematic, classroom, smart class, and holistic activities supplemented by life skills education and an array of video lessons with a Progress Tracker to monitor curriculum coverage. This approach not only aligns with NEP2020 but also ensures that learning is activity-based, enjoyable, and entirely stress-free for your child.

Activities

At The School Of ABC learning is always fun, concepts are always deep-rooted, applications are always encouraged, and creativity is always championed making it the best.

Montessori activities

The Montessori activities in our preschool is a hands-on approach using scientific materials that stimulate creativity in the absorbent minds of children through their exploration of many possibilities and develops the children in 5 key areas of knowledge- Exercises of Practical Life, Sensorial, Language, Maths and Enrichment. Montessori activities stimulate creativity in the absorbent minds of young children and can work wonders in improving organizing skills, patience and concentration levels in young minds.

Thematic activities

Thematic activities strengthen the creative thinking, problem solving ability and develops conversion of thoughts into feelings And feelings are better sensed and remembered forever. Our Thematic teaching covers various age-appropriate topics and stories for children. It is a medium to inculcate moral values, like sharing, respecting & caring among children.

Classroom activities

Classroom activities encompass a variety of engaging tasks such as worksheets, writing exercises, math challenges, drawing, coloring, and interactive class discussions, fostering comprehensive preparation and active participation.
